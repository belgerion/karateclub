Striking Techniques

  • Mountain punch
  • Scissors punch

Blocking Techniques

  • Sweeping block
  • Grasping block
  • Ride hand block
  • Wedging block

Kicking Techniques

  • Hook kick
  • Turning hook kick
  • Front stomping kick
  • Side stomping kick
  • Back stomping kick

Rear Grab Escapes

Throwing Techniques

  • Otoso Gari - major outside reaping
  • Kosoto Gari - minor outside reaping
  • Soto makki komi - outer wrap pull

Uchikomi 6 - 8

  • Right leg front, left jump turning crescent kick, right drop turning sweep kick
  • Right rising block, left pressing kick to right knee, right knee kick to stomach
  • Double jump front kick (block left, kick right)

Kata

    Pinan Godan
  1. Right back stance with left side block
  2. Remain in back stance, right reverse punch
  3. Step in with the right foot to neutral stance with left hook punch and right rear elbow
  4. Shift into left back stance with right side block
  5. Remain in back stance, left reverse punch
  6. Step in with the left foot to neutral stance with right hook punch and left rear elbow
  7. Step into right forward stance with right assisted block
  8. Step in and thru to left forward stance with low X block, immedieately shift into high x block
  9. Twist the wrists and drop hands to stack in cup and saucer, left on top of right, on the right hip
  10. Execute a left knife hand strike to the front
  11. Step forward into right front stance with right lunge punch
  12. Looking over the left shoulder, execute a 180 degree step over turn into a riding stance, with a low right hammer fist
  13. Execute a left knife hand strike to the left side, immediately turn the hand verticle
  14. Execute a right crescent kick, striking the left hand, followed immediately by a right elbow strike to the front striking the left hand
  15. Step behind with the left leg into X stance, with an assisted right block
  16. Step the left leg back and rock back, then shift into an assisted right block
  17. Execute a jump 180 degree spin, landing in an X stance with a low X block
  18. Step forward with the right leg into right forward stance, with an assisted right block
  19. Shift the left foot slightly and twist into right back stance, with a low right spear hand and left cover block
  20. Execute a simultanious low left block and right rising block
  21. Bring left foot back and execute a 180 degree turn, with a right low block and left rising block
  22. Step forward into left back stance, with a low left spear hand and right cover block
  23. Execute a low right block and a left rising block
    Tekki Shodan
  1. Feet together and hands crossed in front of the face
  2. Keeping hands together slowly lower hands to point to the floor
  3. Step left over right into X stance while raising hands to point out
  4. Execute a right inside roundhouse kick, dropping into riding stance
  5. Execute a right ridge hand strike, followed immediately with a left elbow strike into the right palm
  6. Drop hands into cup and saucer on the right hip, followed immediately by a low left block
  7. Execute a right hook punch, ending with the hand still extended
  8. Step right over left into X stance
  9. Execute a left inside roundhouse kick, dropping into riding stance
  10. Execute a right backfist strike
  11. Execute a left straight punch
  12. Execute a right hammer fist strike
  13. Execute a supported left inside block
  14. Execute a left foot waving block, followed immediately with a twist to block the left with the supported left inside block
  15. Execute a right foot wave block, followed by a twist to block the right with the supported inside block
  16. Lower the hands to cup and saucer on the right hip, followed by a double punch to the left with a kiai
